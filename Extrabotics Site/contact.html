Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get involved with Extrabotics? +
We welcome students, professionals, and robotics enthusiasts of all skill levels. You can join our workshops, participate in competitions, or volunteer for community outreach programs. Contact us to learn about current opportunities.
Do you offer educational programs for schools? +
Yes! We provide customized robotics curricula, hands-on workshops, and competition training for K-12 schools and universities. Our programs are designed to inspire the next generation of roboticists and engineers.
San I sponsor or partner with Extrabotics? +
We're always looking for partnerships with organizations that share our vision for robotics innovation. Whether you're interested in sponsoring events, providing equipment, or collaborating on research, we'd love to discuss opportunities.
