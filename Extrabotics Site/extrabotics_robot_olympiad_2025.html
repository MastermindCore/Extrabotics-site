The Extrabotics Robot Olympiad 2025.

The world's most prestigious robotics competition is back! Join us in the ultimate test of innovation, engineering, and competitive spirit.

🎯 Event Overview

The Extrabotics Robot Olympiad 2025 brings together the brightest minds in robotics from universities, research institutions, and schools. This one-day spectacular event features cutting-edge competitions, workshops, and networking opportunities that define the future of robotics.

🏆 Competition Categories

🤖

SoccerBots

Score goals against oponenents in a match of 5 minutes.

🦾

Mission/Maze Bots

Precise object handling and delivery

🚁

Racing Bots

Racing and ground maneuvering challenges
